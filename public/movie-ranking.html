The Best Movies/Web Series of All Time According to Manas

My Top 3 Favorite Movies/Web Series

1. Attack on Titan

It is one of the highest rated anime series of all time. It is a story about humanity's struggle for survival against giants humanoid creatures known as Titans. The series is known for its intense action, complex characters, and thought-provoking themes.

2.Chaava

It is a Hindi Biopic about the son of Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Sambhaji Maharaj. It is a story about the life of Sambhaji Maharaj, his struggles, and his contributions to the Maratha Empire. The movie is known for its powerful performances, stunning visuals, and historical accuracy.

3. The Dark Knight

It is a superhero film that is widely considered one of the best Batman movies ever made. The film is known for its dark and gritty tone, compelling performances, and exceptional cinematography.
